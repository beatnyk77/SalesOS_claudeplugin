# 🛒 E-Commerce Seller Toolkit — Command Reference

Welcome! Here are all available commands in this plugin:

---

## 📦 Product & Listing
**`/product-listing`** — Generate Amazon or Shopify product listing (title, bullets, description, backend keywords)
> Usage: `/product-listing Bamboo Cutting Board | eco-friendly, juice groove, non-slip | home cooks | cutting board bamboo kitchen | amazon`

---

## ⭐ Reviews
**`/review-response`** — Craft a professional response to any review (1–5 stars)
> Usage: `/review-response 2 | "Product broke after a week, very disappointed" | kitchen gadget | BambooHome`

---

## 🔍 Competitive Intelligence
**`/competitor-teardown`** — Analyze a competitor listing and find positioning gaps
> Usage: `/competitor-teardown [paste competitor listing] | My product has lifetime warranty and eco-cert | bamboo cutting board`

---

## 📣 Advertising
**`/ppc-brief`** — Get a complete Amazon PPC campaign structure + bid strategy
> Usage: `/ppc-brief Bamboo Cutting Board | $800/month | 25% ACOS | launch | bamboo cutting board, wood cutting board, kitchen board`

**`/product-launch-ad`** — Generate ad copy for Meta, TikTok, or Google
> Usage: `/product-launch-ad Bamboo Cutting Board | never warp or crack | home cooks 30-55 | meta | 20% off launch`

---

## 🚀 Launch
**`/launch-sequence`** — Write a 5-email product launch campaign
> Usage: `/launch-sequence Bamboo Cutting Board | March 15 | eco-conscious home cooks | 25% | BambooHome`

---

## 🏭 Sourcing
**`/supplier-outreach`** — Write a professional factory/supplier email
> Usage: `/supplier-outreach bamboo cutting board | 500 units | $4.50/unit | initial`

---

## 💡 Pro Tips
- Combine `/competitor-teardown` → `/product-listing` for maximum positioning
- Run `/ppc-brief` before launch so ads are ready on day 1
- Use `/launch-sequence` + `/product-launch-ad` together for a full launch system

Need help? Type `/seller-help` anytime to see this menu.
